Privacy Policy for "Where Am I?"

Last updated: 22/03/2026

1. Introduction

"Where Am I?" helps users view their current geographic information including province/state, city, country, and postal code. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Information

The app collects your precise device location (GPS) only to display your position on Google Maps, determine your address details, and allow you to share this information if you choose. Your location is not stored or sent to any server.

2.2 No Personal Data Collected

The app does not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, device identifiers, or any personal information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your location is used only to show your position on the map, convert coordinates into address information, and allow you to share your location details with others.

4. Sharing of Information

4.1 User-Initiated Sharing

The app allows you to share your location details (state, city, country, postal code) using your device's share menu. Sharing occurs only when you choose to use the Share feature.

4.2 No Automatic Sharing

The app does not automatically share your data with any third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Maps and Google Geocoding services. Google may collect data as described in their Privacy Policy.

Google Maps Attribution

6. Data Storage and Security

No data is stored on servers. All processing happens on your device.

7. Permissions Used

Location Permission: Required to determine your current location and display it on the map.

8. Children's Privacy

The app does not target children and does not collect personal information from users.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
